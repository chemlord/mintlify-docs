## How do I decide which to pick?

Need help navigating the Reaction Tutorial Catalog? We can help. Use the table below to find the section that suits your needs:

|                    | **Tutorial Overview**                                                                 | **Reaction Overview**                                                                                 |
|----------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Purpose**                     | Step-by-step guide to performing reaction mechanisms.                                          | Broad exploration of reactions, including their history, reagents, and interesting facts.            |
| **Focus**                       | Detailed mechanisms and procedural insights.                                                  | Contextual knowledge, reagent preparation, and fun insights.                                         |
| **Best For**                    | Students needing a detailed understanding of how to perform and apply specific reactions.      | Curious learners who want to explore reactions beyond just the mechanism, with engaging extra details. |
| **Example Topics**              | Reaction intermediates, transition states, stereochemistry (if applicable)                                   | Historical significance, reagent sourcing, unusual applications, fun trivia.                         |

Start your journey by choosing a section that aligns with your goals. Happy learning!
